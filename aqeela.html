AQEELA CALISTA

Aqeela Calista is a talented young Indonesian actrees

Aqeela Calista is a multi-talented Indonesian actress, model, and singer who has been active in the entertainment industry since she was a toddler. Known for her expressive acting and charming on-screen presence, she began her career around the age of two and steadily grew into one of the most promising young stars in Indonesia. Her breakthrough came with the television series “Dari Jendela SMP” (2020–2022), where she portrayed Ria, a role that gained her national recognition and a loyal fan base. Since then, Aqeela has starred in numerous popular series, including “Cinta Setelah Cinta,” “Aura,” and most recently, “Asmara Gen Z,” where she plays a lead role in this hit teen drama. Beyond acting, Aqeela is also passionate about music. In 2021, she released her debut single “Cinta Tak Ada yang Tahu,” showcasing her talent as a singer and adding another dimension to her artistic career.
