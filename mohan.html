ARYA MOHAN

Arya Mohan is a talented young Indonesian actor

Arya Mohan is a talented young Indonesian actor who is quickly rising in the entertainment industry. Known for his natural acting skills and charismatic screen presence, Arya began his acting career in 2022 with his debut role as Javit in Catatan Akhir Sekolah the Series. His performance gained attention and opened the door to more prominent projects. In 2023, Arya continued to expand his acting portfolio with appearances in 96 Jam and Galaksi. His breakthrough came with the teen drama series Asmara Gen Z, where he plays one of the leading characters. His portrayal in this series showcased his versatility as an actor and established him as one of the most promising talents of his generation.
